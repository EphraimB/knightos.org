KnightOS

Need help selecting an OS file? Alright, well, pull out your calculator. Above the screen, you should see the model name, for example, "TI-83+". Use this to select a version from the download page.

Once you have an OS file, you need to send it to your calculator. Be warned, however, that this will delete everything on your calculator - including the TIOS archive. If you're ready to go, then grab a copy of TI-Connect or TILP.

Next, open up the batteries on your calculator. Remove a single battery for a moment, then replace it while holding down DEL on the keypad. Plug your calculator into your computer and double click on the OS file you downloaded. Note: You may have to open "TI OS Downloader" instead of double clicking the file.

If all goes well, the software will guide you through the rest of the install. If you have any trouble, feel free to chat with us and ask questions in our IRC channel. We aren't always paying attention, though, so you should ask your question and wait a while for an answer.
