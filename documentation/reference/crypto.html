--- title: Crypto Documentation layout: documentation --- Summary: KnightOS offers minimal cryptographic support, including SHA-1, MD5, and CRC. CRC and MD5 are suitable for data validation, and SHA-1 is suitable for cryptographic hashing. This statement is true in 2014, but may not hold up long after this document is written. Always research the latest news in cryptography before settling on an algorithm.
