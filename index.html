KnightOS An open source OS for TI calculators

KnightOS is a replacement for the stock OS on several Texas Instruments calculators. It provides a Unix-like enviornment that makes it great for students and programmers alike. With advanced features like a tree-based filesystem, multitasking, and more, KnightOS is the perfect operating system for your calculator's needs. Note: KnightOS is not yet complete, and it is not ready for general use. The kernel is nearing completion, though.

  • Multitasking

    Run up to 32 concurrent programs, and switch between them with ease.

  • Terminal

    A built-in bash-like terminal gives more flexibility to power users and programmers.

  • Crash Recovery

    Forget the days of clearing your RAM and losing data. KnightOS crashes are non-destructive.

  • GUI

    Integrated GUI libraries allow programs to easily design complex interfaces.

In addition to the KnightOS userspace, the project includes a seperate kernel that powers all the impressive bits of technology in play here - the filesystem, multitasking, etc, all handled by this kernel. It's not tied to KnightOS at all, and you're free to build your own OSes with this powerful base.
